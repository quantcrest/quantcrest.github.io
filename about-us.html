About Us

S.N. Agarwal & Co. – the parent arm of Quant Crest, is a Jaipur Stock Exchange member since 1992. We have serviced more than 10000+ clients till date with 1000+ clients still active with us currently.

Currently S.N. Agarwal & Co., is an authorized person of BSE, NSE & MCX via our partners – A.C. Agarwal Share Brokers Pvt Ltd & IIFL Securities.


Arun Agarwal

Arun Agarwal, founder of S.N. Agarwal & Co. is a Chartered Accountant & Chartered Secretary. While working as Chartered Secretary for Jaipur Stock Exchange, he purchased membership of Jaipur Stock Exchange and started the brokerage business under S.N. Agarwal & Co in 1996. He has vast experience of 30+ years in financial markets.

Gopal Agarwal

Gopal Agarwal, is a B.Tech (CS) graduate from NMIMS University who found his calling in financial markets thanks to his father’s never ending love with the markets. After graduation, Gopal decided to give up his first job in an IT company for pursuing his dream of working in Tourism industry. He was stationed in Singapore for over 2 years and it was upon his return from Singapore that he had his first interaction with financial markets as he agreed his father’s advice to meet his friend who was mentoring traders from Mumbai under his umbrella. Since then Gopal has worked in various financial institutions such as Sharekhan & PhillipCapital before joining his father at S.N. Agarwal to start a quantitative arm of the company focused on data analysis and automation of trading strategies. Gopal loves analyzing heaps of data to derive a working model based on historical models. He is extremely disciplined about risk management and feels the holy grail, if any, is indeed risk management.
